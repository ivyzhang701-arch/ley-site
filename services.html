Our services, by category.

A structured view of the work LEY takes on, with the specific hours we hold for each. Every session is billed by the hour in thirty-minute increments.

Five categories

What we take on, and when.

  1. Personal Styling & Wardrobe

    $180 / hr · 2 hr min

    Seasonal capsule planning, private showroom appointments, and on-site closet edits.

    Clients are matched with houses from a short list the studio has cultivated over a decade. Appointments are unhurried and arrive without retail pressure.

    • Hours of operation
    • Private showroom visitsTuesday – Friday   10:00 – 18:00
    • At-home closet editsMon & Wed   13:00 – 19:00
    • Runway season reviewFirst Saturday · Feb & Sep   14:00 – 17:00
    • Virtual styling sessionsWeekdays by appointment   20:00 – 22:00 ET

  2. Luxury Travel Planning

    $220 / hr · 30-min blocks

    Destination research, itinerary building, and on-trip concierge support through vetted partners.

    Planning happens across two or three short meetings. Trips are booked through partners whose first loyalty is to your experience, not a commission structure.

    • Hours of operation
    • Planning consultationsMon · Wed · Fri   09:00 – 12:00
    • Partner-led briefingsTuesday   15:00 – 18:00
    • Live-trip concierge24 hours while clients are in motion
    • Post-trip debriefWithin 10 business days of return

  3. Residence & Move-in

    $200 / hr · travel quoted

    Relocation support, furniture sourcing, vendor liaison, and the unglamorous details that make a house feel like home.

    Long engagements at a steady cadence — often two days a week for a season. Follow-through is contracted upfront so the first month after move-in is not scrambled.

    • Hours of operation
    • Site walkthroughsMon & Thu   10:00 – 16:00
    • Vendor interview daysLast Friday monthly   09:00 – 14:00
    • Move-day oversightFlexible, including weekends
    • 30-day follow-throughEvery Wednesday afternoon · 1 hour

  4. Wellness & Recovery

    $160 / hr · partner fees separate

    Booking private instructors, arranging retreats, and coordinating in-home recovery teams.

    Partner fees remain separate and transparent. LEY does not receive a margin on treatments, retreats, or instructor time.

    • Hours of operation
    • Retreat discovery callsTue & Thu   11:00 – 13:00
    • Private instructor introsSaturday   09:00 – 12:00
    • At-home recovery coordinationSame-day   08:00 – 20:00
    • Quarterly wellness reviewSecond week of each quarter

  5. Brand & Image Advisory

    $300 / hr · 6-week min

    Strategic image direction for founders, artists, and private principals entering a new public chapter.

    A six-week minimum engagement with two working sessions each week. Photo-day presence is billed at a day rate agreed upfront.

    • Hours of operation
    • Intake interviewMonday   14:00 – 17:00
    • Working sessionsTue & Thu   09:30 – 12:30
    • Photo-day presenceBy arrangement · day rate
    • Quarterly refreshFirst Friday · Jan · Apr · Jul · Oct   10:00 – 13:00
